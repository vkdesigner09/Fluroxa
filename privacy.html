Privacy Policy

Fluroxa Technologies Pvt. Ltd.  ·  Effective: 29 May 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 June 2026

Plain language first: We collect only what we need to run your demo and deliver the product. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. You can ask us to delete everything at any time.

1. Who We Are

Fluroxa is a financial analytics platform built for finance teams across India. We are operated by Fluroxa Technologies Pvt. Ltd., incorporated under the Companies Act 2013.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you visit our website, submit a demo request, or use our platform. It applies to all visitors, prospects, and customers.

2. Data We Collect

Data you give us directly — when you submit the demo request form:

  • Full name
  • Work email address
  • Company name
  • Company size (optional)
  • Phone number (optional)

Data collected automatically — when you visit our website:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL
  • IP address (anonymised after 24 hours)

We do not collect financial data, transaction records, or any data from your accounting tools unless you actively connect them as a paying customer — and only with your explicit consent at the time of connection.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you submit for the following purposes only:

  • To schedule and deliver your requested demo
  • To follow up about your Fluroxa subscription, if you choose to proceed
  • To send product updates — only if you opt in
  • To improve the website and onboarding experience based on aggregate, never individual, analytics

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party.

4. How We Store Your Data

Demo request data submitted via our website is stored in a secure internal system accessible only to our onboarding team. All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256. Data in transit is protected using TLS 1.2 or higher.

Once you become a customer, your account data is hosted on cloud infrastructure with servers located in India or within jurisdictions that provide equivalent data protection standards.

Access to your data within our team is role-based — only personnel who need it to deliver your demo or manage your account can access it.

5. Third-Party Processors

We use a limited number of third-party services. Each is bound by data processing agreements:

  • Google Workspace — Internal communication and document management, processed under Google's standard DPA
  • Cloud hosting provider — Secure infrastructure for platform data. Details available on request
  • Analytics — Anonymised, aggregated website usage data only. No personal identifiers are shared

We do not integrate with advertising networks, data brokers, or social media tracking pixels.

6. Data Retention

Demo requests: Retained for 12 months from submission, or until you ask us to delete it — whichever comes first.

Active customer accounts: Retained for the duration of your subscription.

After cancellation: Your data is retained for 30 days so you can export it. After 30 days, it is permanently deleted from all our systems. We will confirm deletion on request.

7. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act 2023

As a data principal under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Right to grievance redressal — Raise a complaint and receive a response within 30 days
  • Right to nominate — Nominate another person to exercise your rights in the event of your incapacity

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our website uses strictly necessary cookies to function correctly. We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking cookies, or third-party cookie networks.

If we introduce optional analytics cookies in future, we will ask for your consent before setting them.

9. Children's Privacy

Fluroxa is a B2B product designed for working professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently done so, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will notify active customers by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect. The "Last updated" date at the top always reflects the most recent version.
